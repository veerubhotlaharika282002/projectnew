About Us

We are Professional Teams to help the poor

The YourLocal Army is a zero-funds volunteer organization that works to get surplus food from restaurants and communities to serve the less fortunate. Our Locals are largely students and young working professionals ‐ everyone does this in their free time. The lesser fortunate sections of society we serve include homeless families, orphanages, patients from public hospitals and old age homes.
